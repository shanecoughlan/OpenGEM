|DESCRIPTION|Epson GQ-3500 Laser Printer (300 x 300 Dots/Inch)
|SHORT DESCR|EPSON GQ 3500

|FNT WILDCRD|*.B30
|AUX FILE|EGH.ATM
|SRC DISK|GEM PRINTER DISK 3
|LONG DESCRP|

printer.  This printer offers a print resolution of 300 x 300

|DESCRIPTION|LaserWriter / Postscript Printers

|SRC DISK|GEM PRINTER DISK 2
|AUX FILE|PSTV23.PRE;PSTV23.CNF
|LONG DESCRP|
Choose this entry if you are using a printer that uses Postscript-type 
files and the standard Adobe resident font set: Courier, Helvetica, 
Times, and Symbol.

|FILENAME|PDPST8.SYS
|SRC DISK|GEM PRINTER DISK 2
|AUX FILE|PST.PRE;PST.CNF
|LONG DESCRP|

Postscript-type files and has the extended Adobe resident font

resident font set: Courier, Helvetica, Times, and Symbol.
